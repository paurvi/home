Paurvi Shinde

I am currently persuing my PhD in Immunology at University of Connecticut Health under guidance of Dr. Anthony Vella. My research here focuses on understanding the signaling mechanisms induced by different TLR adjuvants when combined with OX40 costimulation in order to enhance the Th1 responses. Research outcomes can be applied in designing an optimal vaccine against certain infectious diseases where Th1 responses are crucial.

Before this I have completed my M.S in Biotechnology from Texas Tech University and I worked for an year in Immunotherapeutics Department of Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center. Here i studied the pathogensis of Tuberous Sclerosis Complex, a multisystem tumor suppressor gene syndrome.

After my PhD, I am interested in working on research that focuses on optimizing vaccines against infectious diseases that pose a great socioeconomic burden. Apart from research I enjoy certain hobbies, that I have mentioned in miscellaneous section.
